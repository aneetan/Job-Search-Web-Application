FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions about our job search application!

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Login" button located in the top-right corner of the page. Then, select "Create Account" and follow the prompts to enter your personal details and required information.
How do I apply for a job?
To apply for a job, first, make sure you are logged in to your account. Then, navigate to the "Available Jobs" section, browse the listings, and click on the job you're interested in. Finally, click the "Apply" button and follow the instructions to complete your application.
Can I upload my CV/Resume?
Yes, you can upload your CV/Resume to your profile. After logging in, go to the "CV/Resume" section and follow the instructions to upload your professional achievements, goals, and awards.
Can I add a job description representing company?
Yes, just click on the Hire on the navbar, register if you are new and you can add jobs with descriptions and other attributes
